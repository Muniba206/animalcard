Penguin

Penguins are flightless birds. While other birds have wings for flying, penguins have adapted flippers to help them swim in the water.

  • Scienific Name: Spheniscidae
  • Average Length: 120 centimeter
  • Average Lifespan: 6 years
  • Habitat: Tropical Islands

Penguins are a group of aquatic, flightless birds. They live almost exclusively in the Southern Hemisphere.
